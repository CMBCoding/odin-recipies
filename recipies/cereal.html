Cereal

Description

A breakfast staple so easy to make, a monkey could do it.

Ingredients

  • Cereal - 1 box
  • Milk - 2 cups

Steps

  1. Get a bowl and a spoon.
  2. Open cereal box.
  3. Pour cereal into bowl.
  4. Pour milk into bowl.
  5. Stir with spoon.
  6. (Optional) let sit for a minute or two for the cereal to absorb the milk to become softer.
  7. Serve.
