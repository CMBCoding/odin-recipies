Chicken Katsu

Description

A deep-fried Japanese style chicken cutlet that is delicious and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts - 2-3
  • Teriyaki Sauce - 1 cup
  • Flour - 150grams
  • Panko - 100grams
  • Egg - 1 large
  • Cooking oil - 2 cups
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Using a knife and cutting board, lightly score the chicken breasts.
  2. Pour the sauce into a zip-lock baggie with the chicken breasts.
  3. Seal the bag, shake well, set in the refridgerator to marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. While the chick is marinating, add the oil into a sufficiently deep pot (prefferably) or pan and set heat to medium-high.
  5. Get two sheet pans or similar containers. Pour the flour into one, Panko into the other. Spread evenly.
  6. Mix the pepper in with the flour.
  7. Get a bowl, crack and whisk the egg.
  8. Retrieve the chicken. Drain sauce, pat down with paper towels.
  9. Dredge chicken in four, coat in egg, then dredge in the Panko before placing onto a platter.
  10. Place the chicken into the oil. Fry for 8 minutes or until golden brown, turning as needed
  11. Remove from the oil, let the excess drain or be seeped into paper towels.
  12. Serve.
