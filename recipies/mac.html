Mac & Cheese

Description

A classic pasta and cheese dish made simple.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni - 8 ounces
  • Butter - 1/4 cup
  • Flour - 1/4 cup
  • Milk - 2 cups
  • Shredded Cheese - 2 cups
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. If not using store-bought pre-shredded cheese, grate cheese into shreds.
  2. Bring a pot of water to boil. Cook the macaroni until soft, for about 8 minutes.
  3. Start on the roux. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Add flour, salt, pepper and stir until smooth.
  4. Slowly pour in milk and stir until the mixture is smooth and bubbling. Take care not to burn the milk.
  5. Slowly stir in the cheese until it is melted.
  6. Once the macaroni is finished cooking, drain, and stir into the cheese sauce until coated.
  7. Serve.
